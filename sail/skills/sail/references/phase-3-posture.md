## Phase 3: Agentic Posture Management (Build) 

The Build phase performs a deep posture analysis of the agents and AI assets identified in Discovery. It involves understanding, mapping, and graphing the agent landscape — identity, tool and connector scopes, memory and RAG dependencies, A2A and MCP edges, and the platforms underneath — to establish a clear picture of the system's security posture and the attack paths that span it.
